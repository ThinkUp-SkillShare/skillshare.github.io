About us

Transforming the future of collaborative education, one connection at a time

At ThinkUp, we believe that the education of the future is collaborative, inclusive, and powered by technology.

Who Are We?

We are ThinkUp, a startup founded by students passionate about educational innovation and technology, committed to transforming the way young people learn and collaborate.

We believe that knowledge grows when it's shared, which is why we developed SkillShare, a social networking app focused on connecting students with similar academic interests to form dynamic and effective study groups.

Our Mission

To promote collaborative learning through technology by providing students with a platform that connects people with the same academic goals, encourages cooperation, and facilitates access to shared study resources to improve academic performance.

Our Vision

To become the leading educational social network in Latin America and around the world, where millions of students find in SkillShare a safe, dynamic, and trustworthy space to learn together, enhance their abilities, and build strong academic communities that go beyond the classroom.

Our Values

  • Collaboration: We believe that knowledge multiplies when it's shared, and we work to strengthen cooperation among students.
  • Innovation: We are committed to using technology to transform the way people learn and connect academically.
  • Accessibility: We strive to ensure that all students, regardless of their background or socioeconomic status, can access quality tools to improve their learning.
  • Commitment to Education: We are driven by the continuous improvement of the educational process, promoting both personal and collective development.
